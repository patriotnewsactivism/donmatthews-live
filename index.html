Why I built all of this

I'm a Marine veteran and civil rights journalist. I've seen what happens when people don't have access to the right tools — they lose. They lose their rights. They lose their cases. They lose their voice.

So I started building. Not because I had funding, not because a VC told me to. Because the tools the powerful use shouldn't be exclusive to the powerful.

WTP News. Civil Rights Hub. ChatScream. CaseBuddy. BuildMyBot. CodeForge. TubeScribe. Every single one built with one question in mind: "What does the average person actually need?"
